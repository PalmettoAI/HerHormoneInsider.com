About

The premise: women deserve journalism about their own bodies

Her Hormone Insider is an independent editorial publication covering hormone health for women aged 35 to 60. We are reader-supported. We are clinician-reviewed. We do not accept pharmaceutical advertising.

Editorial standards

Every article is written by a journalist with a named byline and reviewed by at least one board-certified clinician with relevant specialty training (OB/GYN, endocrinology, internal medicine with menopause certification). Reviewers' names appear on each article. Reviewer relationships and any conflicts are disclosed at the bottom of the article.

Our default standard for medical claims is peer-reviewed evidence published in journals indexed by PubMed, supplemented by guidance from NAMS, the Endocrine Society, ACOG, and equivalent international bodies (BMS, IMS). Where the evidence is mixed or evolving, we say so. Where guidelines disagree, we explain the disagreement.

Medical reviewers

Our standing reviewer panel includes board-certified physicians in OB/GYN, reproductive endocrinology, family medicine with menopause focus, and integrative medicine. Each reviewer is paid a flat editorial fee for their time. None hold equity in any telehealth platform we cover.

Affiliate and revenue disclosure

This site is reader-supported and earns revenue from three sources: (1) reader subscriptions to the weekly dispatch, (2) a small affiliate commission from some — not all — telehealth providers when readers begin care through our links, and (3) the sale of independently produced research reports.

Affiliate revenue does not influence the comparison scoring methodology described on the provider comparison page. We have, multiple times, scored non-affiliated providers above affiliated ones. The scoring weights are public; the spreadsheet is shared with our editorial board quarterly.

We do not accept pharmaceutical advertising, paid sponsorship of articles, or paid placements in any form. We have declined offers from each.

Contact

Editorial inquiries, corrections, and sources: editors@herhormoneinsider.com. We acknowledge correspondence within 72 hours and publish all corrections with a dated note at the top of the affected article.
